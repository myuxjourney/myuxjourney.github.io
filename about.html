As a new UX Designer now honing my talents through an apprenticeship, I find myself totally immersed in the world of design. My path began in the field of graphic design, where I spent over five years perfecting the art of visual communication.This encounter reinforced my opinion that, while decent design may sell a product, great design has the ability to inspire belief and elicit emotions. Aside from my professional projects, I am an avid sports fan and have a special interest in photography and artistic outlets, which allow me to express myself in a variety of ways. A profound respect for meaningful and impactful design is central to my work and hobbies, and it serves as a guiding principle in both my professional and personal life.
